PhD Candidate in Computational Science

Biography

Theron Guo is an enthusiastic computational scientist, who is exploring statistical methods for challenging nonlinear problems in the field of continuum mechanics. He is currently working as a PhD Candidate in reduced order modeling for multi-scale problems under the supervision of Prof. Karen Veroy and Dr. Ondřej Rokoš.
